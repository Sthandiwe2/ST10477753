1. Organization Description
Lionel’s Tech Support is an imagined IT support company that focuses on assisting individuals and small businesses with everyday technical problems. The organization provides troubleshooting services, user guidance, and digital support through an interactive website platform. The platform allows users to access a knowledge base for common issues or submit support tickets for more complex problems. 

2. Reasons for Website Choice
The decision to create Lionel’s Tech Support was based on the need to solve real-life technology problems that many people experience daily. Technology is widely used in modern society; however not all users have the knowledge or skills to resolve technical issues independently. 
Many individuals, students and small businesses often face challenges such as Wi-Fi connectivity issues, malfunctioning printers, forgotten passwords, and slow computer performance. These issues, although common, can cause frustration, reduce productivity and waste valuable time
This website was chosen to provide a structured and accessible platform where users can find quick solutions or request assistance without relying on unverified sources or expensive technicians

3. Goals
The main goal of the website is to provide a reliable and user-friendly platform for technical support. It aims to simplify problem-solving by offering clear guidance and accessible assistance to users with varying levels of technical knowledge. 
Another goal is to improve customer experience by ensuring that support services are organized, efficient, and easy to access through a centralized system. 

4. Objectives 
•	To develop an interactive website with a functional knowledge base
•	To implement a ticketing system that assigns unique IDs to user issues
•	To provide step-by-step solutions for common technical problems.
•	To improve response time and communication between users and support agents
•	To ensure the website is simple, clear, and easy to navigate. 

5. Timeline and Milestones
 The development of the Lionel’s Tech Support website took place over three weeks, with each week dedicated to specific stages of the project to ensure steady progress and successful completion.

•	Week 1: Planning and Research
During the first week, the main idea of the website was established and explored in detail. Research was carried out to understand common technical issues faced by users, and a basic structure for the website was outlined. Important features, such as knowledge base and ticketing system, were also identified and planned. 

•	Week 2: Design and Development
In the second week, the visual layout and structure of the website were created. Key pages including Home, About, Services, Knowledge Base, and Contact pages, were developed. In addition, relevant website assets such as images and branding elements were incorporated, while core features like the support system were built.

•	Week 3: Testing and Finalization
The final week focused on reviewing the website to detect and correct any errors. Adjustments were made to improve usability and overall performance. After completing the testing process, the websites were finalized and prepared for submission or launch.
